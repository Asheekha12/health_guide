What is PCOS?

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a common hormonal disorder among women of reproductive age. It can cause irregular periods, acne, excessive hair growth, and difficulty in pregnancy.

Prevention & Lifestyle Tips

  • Maintain a healthy weight through diet and exercise.
  • Manage your blood sugar levels with a low-glycemic diet.
  • Get regular exercise to help regulate your hormones.
  • Consider talking to a healthcare provider for personalized advice.
